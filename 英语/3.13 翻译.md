# 📝 CET-6 翻译实战精析：自强自立精神

---

## 一、原题

中华民族崇尚自强自立的精神，这一精神深植于中国文化之中，是民族生存与发展的重要支撑。改革开放以来，中国在自强自立的征程上，持续加大科研投入，在信息技术、人工智能、航天工程等领域不断取得新突破。5G 网络覆盖全国，远程医疗发展迅速，电子支付的使用全球领先，嫦娥探月工程展现了航天领域引人注目的发展。自强自主精神成为推动国家现代化进程的强大动力，激励着中国人民为实现民族复兴（rejuvenation）的中国梦不断奋进。

---

## 二、原始译文（练习版）

> China people advocate the spirit of self-reliance, which has been deeply rooted in Chinese culture, and it is an important support of people living and development. From the reform and opening up, China continuously improved investment in science on the journey of self-reliance, and continually make new breakthroughs in information technology, artificial intelligence and space engineering. 5G net covers the whole country, remote medical care develops in a high speed, the usage of e-pay leads the world, and the Chang-e moon exploration project shows remarkable progress in space. The spirit of self-reliance has become a strong driving force of pushing the process of modernization, and inspired China people strive ahead continuously for the China dream of national rejuvenation.

## 三、修补后的完整译文（Final Polished Version）

The Chinese nation cherishes the spirit of self-reliance and self-improvement, a spirit that is deeply rooted in Chinese culture and serves as a vital pillar for the nation's survival and development. Since the reform and opening-up, China has continuously increased its investment in scientific research on the journey toward self-reliance, constantly making new breakthroughs in fields such as information technology, artificial intelligence, and aerospace engineering. With 5G networks covering the entire country and telemedicine developing rapidly, the world-leading adoption of electronic payment and the Chang'e lunar exploration program have demonstrated remarkable progress in the aerospace sector. The spirit of self-reliance has become a powerful driving force for advancing the country's modernization, inspiring the Chinese people to forge ahead ceaselessly in pursuit of the Chinese Dream of national rejuvenation.

## 五、深度词汇解析（Vocab Deep Dive）

### 1. Cherish

- **音标**：/ˈtʃerɪʃ/
- **词类**：v.（及物动词）
- **释义**：珍视；怀有（信念、希望）
- **搭配**：cherish a belief / tradition / memory
- **例句**：We should cherish the traditions passed down by our ancestors.
- **译文**：我们应当珍视先辈传承下来的传统。

### 2. Rooted

- **音标**：/ˈruːtɪd/
- **词类**：adj.
- **释义**：根深蒂固的；深植于……的
- **搭配**：be deeply rooted in（深植于……）
- **例句**：His suspicion of strangers is deeply rooted in childhood experiences.
- **译文**：他对陌生人的疑虑深深植根于童年经历。

### 3. Breakthrough

- **音标**：/ˈbreɪkθruː/
- **词类**：n.
- **释义**：突破；重大进展
- **搭配**：make / achieve a breakthrough；a major breakthrough in…
- **例句**：Scientists are on the verge of a major breakthrough in cancer research.
- **译文**：科学家们在癌症研究方面即将取得重大突破。

### 4. Remarkable

- **音标**：/rɪˈmɑːrkəbl/
- **词类**：adj.
- **释义**：非凡的；引人注目的；卓越的
- **搭配**：a remarkable achievement / progress
- **例句**：The country has made remarkable progress in poverty alleviation.
- **译文**：该国在扶贫方面取得了显著成就。

### 5. Rejuvenation

- **音标**：/rɪˌdʒuːvəˈneɪʃn/
- **词类**：n.
- **释义**：复兴；恢复活力
- **搭配**：national rejuvenation（民族复兴）
- **例句**：The city is undergoing a period of economic rejuvenation.
- **译文**：这座城市正在经历一段经济复兴时期。

### 6. Ceaselessly

- **音标**：/ˈsiːsləsli/
- **词类**：adv.
- **释义**：不停地；不断地
- **搭配**：work / strive ceaselessly
- **例句**：The researchers worked ceaselessly to find a solution.
- **译文**：研究人员不懈努力以寻找解决方案。

## 七、互动问答复盘（Q&A Review）

**Q1："中华民族"能翻译成 "China people" 吗？**

> 不可以。"China people" 存在语法错误（China 为名词，不能直接修饰名词），且语体过于随意。六级翻译中"中华民族"首选 **The Chinese nation**；泛指"中国人民"时使用 **the Chinese people**。

**Q2：为什么"加大投入"不宜用 "improve investment"？**

> *Improve* 意为"改善质量"，侧重定性提升；而"加大投入"强调的是**数量上的增加**，应使用 **increase** 或 **scale up**。

**Q3：遇到 "Since…" 开头的句子，时态应注意什么？**

> 这是六级高频考点。*Since* 引导时间状语时，主句须用**现在完成时（have/has + done）**，表示"从过去某一时刻延续至今"的动作或状态。

**Q4：如何避免句子冗长、过度使用 "and"？**

> 可采用以下策略：
> - **非限制性定语从句**（…, which…）整合补充信息
> - **With 复合结构**（With sth. doing/done…）表伴随状态
> - **现在分词短语**作伴随状语，将次要信息降级
