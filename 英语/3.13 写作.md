## 📝 题目

**Writing (30 minutes)**

Copy and begin your essay with the following sentence exactly:

> "Living in an increasingly competitive world, college students should better prepare themselves to confront various challenges."

- **Time allowed:** 30 minutes
- **Length:** 150–200 words
- **Content:** You may make comments, cite examples, or use personal experiences to develop your essay.

---

## ✏️ 原文

Living in an increasingly competitive world, college students should better prepare themselves to confront various challenges. As time changes, students would face more and more pressure, such as study, life, job and so on. And the world has become more competitive, too.

So how could college students face pressure and challenges? Firstly, students should develop hard skills. For example, office software mastery is easy but very useful, and is one of the hard skills that is essential for searching job. Then, students should enhance soft skills like mental toughness. Mental toughness is essential to deal with issues in today's world, and a good mental health is important. Last but not least, in order to integrate into society, students should gain more social experience. More social experience means more practical solutions beyond textbooks, and means more options when one path closes.

In short, college students should improve themselves to confront pressure and challenges. With well-prepared skills, college students will have bright future.

---

## ✅ 修改版

**Living in an increasingly competitive world, college students should better prepare themselves to confront various challenges.** As society evolves, students face mounting pressure from academics, personal lives, and career prospects. To thrive in such a demanding environment, proactive preparation is essential.

Firstly, developing hard skills is paramount. For instance, **proficiency in office software** is not only a boost to efficiency but also **indispensable for job hunting**. Secondly, students should enhance soft skills, particularly mental toughness. This quality is essential to **navigate the complexities** of today's world, while maintaining good mental health serves as a **prerequisite** for coping with setbacks. Last but not least, gaining social experience is vital. It **translates into practical solutions** beyond textbooks and **provides a wider range of options** when one path closes.

In conclusion, by proactively sharpening these skills and maintaining a resilient mindset, college students will be well-equipped to confront any challenge and secure a bright future.

## 🔍 深度复盘：为什么要这么改？

在六级考场上，考官每份卷子只看几十秒。你要做的不是写"对"，而是写出**"高级感"**。

比如，当你把 `means more options` 改成 `provides a wider range of options` 时，你不仅仅是换了词，你是在展示你具备**精确描述抽象概念**的能力。

## 📖 词汇解析

### 1. Paramount

- **音标：** /ˈpærəmaʊnt/
- **词类：** adj.
- **释义：** 至为重要的，首要的。
- **搭配：** of paramount importance（至关重要）；paramount goal（首要目标）
- **例句：** In the face of fierce competition, developing professional skills is paramount for college students.
- **译文：** 面对激烈的竞争，对大学生来说，发展专业技能是至关重要的。

### 2. Proficiency

- **音标：** /prəˈfɪʃnsi/
- **词类：** n.
- **释义：** 熟练，精通。
- **搭配：** language proficiency（语言熟练度）；proficiency in office software（精通办公软件）
- **例句：** Proficiency in digital tools has become a basic requirement in the modern workplace.
- **译文：** 熟练掌握数字工具已成为现代职场的一项基本要求。

### 3. Indispensable

- **音标：** /ˌɪndɪˈspensəbl/
- **词类：** adj.
- **释义：** 必不可少的，不可或缺的。
- **搭配：** an indispensable part（不可或缺的一部分）；indispensable for success（成功的必要条件）
- **例句：** Practical experience is indispensable for students to bridge the gap between theory and reality.
- **译文：** 实践经验对于学生弥合理论与现实之间的差距是必不可少的。

### 4. Navigate

- **音标：** /ˈnævɪɡeɪt/
- **词类：** v.
- **释义：** 找到方向；应对（复杂局面）。
- **搭配：** navigate the complexities（应对复杂局面）；navigate a career path（规划职业路径）
- **例句：** The ability to navigate challenges effectively is what distinguishes successful leaders.
- **译文：** 有效应对挑战的能力是成功领导者的标志。

### 5. Prerequisite

- **音标：** /ˌpriːˈrekwəzɪt/
- **词类：** n.
- **释义：** 先决条件，前提。
- **搭配：** a prerequisite for success（成功的先决条件）；meet the prerequisites（满足前提条件）
- **例句：** Maintaining a positive mindset is a prerequisite for overcoming life's obstacles.
- **译文：** 保持积极的心态是克服生活障碍的前提条件。

### 6. Resilient

- **音标：** /rɪˈzɪliənt/
- **词类：** adj.
- **释义：** 有弹性的；能复原的；适应力强的。
- **搭配：** resilient mindset（坚韧的心态）；resilient economy（有韧性的经济）
- **例句：** Students need to be resilient enough to handle setbacks in their job hunting process.
- **译文：** 学生需要有足够的韧性来应对求职过程中的挫折。
