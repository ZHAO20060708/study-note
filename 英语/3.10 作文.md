## 题目

**Directions:** For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay that begins with the sentence *"It is believed that teachers can exert a profound influence on their students' academic pursuit and personal development."* You can make comments, cite examples or use your personal experiences to develop your essay. You should write at least 150 words but no more than 200 words.

## 原文

It is believed that teachers can exert a profound influence on their students' academic pursuit and personal development. In my opinion, a good teacher will have significantly good impact on students, and a bad teacher is not only bad in education but also can have negative impact.

Education is not only teachers gives lectures to student. It can have positive or negative impact on students. For example, a good teacher can give more support to students and will have a positive impact on the increase of student. In contrast, a bad teacher often give student negative evaluations, and they can not give student any help but slower their improvement.

In short, a good teacher is very essential for student. The influence of teacher can not be ignored because they will have great impact in the future. As students in college, we should cherish good education sources and find good teachers in order to improve academic pursuit and personal development.

## 修改版（186 words）

"It is believed that teachers can exert a profound influence on their students' academic pursuit and personal development." From my perspective, an exceptional educator acts as a cornerstone of a student's growth, whereas an incompetent one may leave lasting scars on their future path.

To begin with, true education transcends the mere delivery of lectures. It is a dual-edged sword that can shape a student's destiny in opposite ways. For instance, a supportive teacher can ignite a student's passion for learning, thereby fostering their all-around development. In sharp contrast, a discouraging teacher often offers negative evaluations, which not only hinders a student's progress but may also stifle their innate potential. Such negative interactions can extinguish the spark of curiosity that is vital for academic success.

In short, the presence of a virtuous mentor is of paramount importance. Their influence is so far-reaching that it can mold a student's character and worldview. As college students, we should actively seek guidance from quality educational sources and cherish mentors who can truly pave the way for our academic excellence and personal maturity.
