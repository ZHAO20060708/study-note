Writing (30 minutes)

Prompt

- Copy and begin your essay with the following sentence exactly:
- “Living in an increasingly competitive world, college students should better prepare themselves to confront various challenges.”

Requirements

- Time allowed: 30 minutes.
- Length: 150–200 words.
- Content: You may make comments, cite examples, or use personal experiences to develop your essay.

Essay (start here)

Living in an increasingly competitive world, college students should better prepare themselves to confront various challenges. As time changes, students would face more and more pressure, such as study, life, job and so on. And the world has become more competitive, too. 

So how could college students face pressure and challenges? Firstly, students should develop hard skills. For example, office software mastery is easy but very useful, and is one of the hard skills that is essential for searching job. Then, students should enhance soft skills like mental toughness. Mental toughness is essential to deal with issues in today's world, and a good mental health is important. Last but not least, in order to integrate into society, students should gain more social experience. More social experience means more practical solutions beyond textbooks, and means more options when one path closes.

In short, college students should improve themselves to confront pressure and challenges. With well-prepared skills, college students will have bright future.
