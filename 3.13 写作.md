Writing (30 minutes)
Directions: For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay that begins with the sentence “Living in an
increasingly competitive world, college students should better prepare themselves to confront various challenges.”
You can make comments, cite examples or use your personal experiences to develop your essay. You should write at
least 150 words but no more than 200 words.
You should copy the sentence given in quotes at the beginning of your essay.
