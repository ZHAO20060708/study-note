## 一、题目

**Directions:** For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay that begins with the sentence *"As social media is used more and more extensively, there is a growing awareness of the importance of using it properly and responsibly."* You can make comments, cite examples or use your personal experiences to develop your essay. You should write at least 150 words but no more than 200 words.

> ⚠️ You should **copy** the sentence given in quotes at the beginning of your essay.

---

## 二、原文

As social media is used more and more extensively, there is a growing awareness of the importance of using it properly and responsibly. As the technology progressing rapidly, the social media is more and more popular among people. However, problems have emerged along with it.

Then, how can we use these platforms properly and responsibly? Firstly, the authorities should develop relevant rules and laws to strictly regulate online behaviors. Secondly, as individuals, we must protect our personal privacy. Last but not least, we should always keep our eyes open and think independently to distinguish fake news from facts before sharing anything online.

In short, as college students, we should take the lead to use social media wisely and responsibly. Only by doing so can we truly enjoy the convenience of modern technology without falling into its hidden traps.

## 三、修订终稿

> **词数：162 词** ✅

As social media is used more and more extensively, there is a growing awareness of the importance of using it properly and responsibly. With the rapid advancement of technology, social media has permeated virtually every aspect of our daily lives. Yet, this digital revolution is not without its pitfalls, making it crucial to use these platforms wisely.

To begin with, it is imperative that authorities formulate stringent regulations to curb online misconduct and safeguard the digital ecosystem. Furthermore, as individuals, we must remain vigilant about personal privacy and avoid oversharing sensitive information on public platforms. Most importantly, we should cultivate critical thinking to distinguish truth from falsehood amid the daily deluge of online information.

In conclusion, as contemporary college students, we are expected to embrace social media with a rational and responsible mindset. Only by doing so can we truly harness the convenience of modern technology without falling into its hidden traps.

## 四、原文 → 修改版 逐项对照

| 维度 | 原文 | 修改版 | 提升要点 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **语法纠错** | `As the technology progressing rapidly` | `With the rapid advancement of technology` | 原文为悬垂分词（缺谓语），改用 `With + 名词短语` 结构 |
| **冠词修正** | `the social media is more and more popular` | `social media has permeated...` | social media 为不可数名词，通常零冠词；同时消除 `more and more` 的口语化重复 |
| **衔接词升级** | `Firstly / Secondly / Last but not least` | `To begin with / Furthermore / Most importantly` | 序数词连接偏模板化；替换后递进感更强，符合学术写作 |
| **句式客观化** | `we must protect our personal privacy` | `we must remain vigilant about personal privacy` | 引入 `remain vigilant`（保持警觉），比 `protect` 更具学术精确性 |

## 五、写作规范与策略分析

### 1. 论证逻辑的严密性

- **原文**：简单序数词引导（`Firstly / Secondly / Last but not least`），论点平行罗列，缺乏层次递进。
- **修改版**：使用 `To begin with / Furthermore / Most importantly` 构建**由外而内**的递进结构——
  - **制度层面**（政府监管）→ **个体行为**（隐私保护）→ **思维能力**（批判性思考）
  - 逻辑链条逐层深入，论证力度依次增强。

### 2. 句式结构的复杂性与客观性

- **原文**：倾向于第一人称 `We should...`（主观性较强）。
- **修改版**：引入以下手法使表述更为客观、中立：
  - **形式主语**：`It is imperative that...`
  - **被动语态**：`we are expected to...`
  - **部分倒装**：`Only by doing so can we...`（结尾保留，显著提升修辞力度）

### 3. 词汇选用的精准性

- **原文**：使用含义较广的常用动词（`use / protect / stop`）。
- **修改版**：采用具备特定学术语义的动词（`harness / cultivate / curb`）。精准的用词能够更细腻地传达作者意图，同时向阅卷人展示词汇深度。

## 六、核心词汇深度解析

### 1. Imperative

- **音标**：/ɪmˈperətɪv/
- **词类**：adj. / n.
- **释义**：至关重要的，迫切的；必要的事
- **搭配**：`a moral imperative`（道德准则）；`it is imperative to address...`（解决……是迫切的）
- **例句**：It is **imperative** that students develop robust digital literacy to thrive in the information age.
- **译文**：在信息时代，学生培养扎实的数字素养是至关重要的。

### 2. Stringent

- **音标**：/ˈstrɪndʒənt/
- **词类**：adj.
- **释义**：严厉的，（标准、规则等）极严格的
- **搭配**：`stringent criteria`（严格标准）；`adhere to stringent safety protocols`（遵守严格的安全协议）
- **例句**：The implementation of **stringent** oversight is necessary to prevent the exploitation of user data.
- **译文**：为了防止用户数据被滥用，实施严格的监督是必要的。

### 3. Vigilant

- **音标**：/ˈvɪdʒɪlənt/
- **词类**：adj.
- **释义**：警觉的，保持警惕的
- **名词形式**：vigilance /ˈvɪdʒɪləns/
- **搭配**：`remain vigilant about / against...`（对……保持警惕）；`maintain constant vigilance`（保持高度警觉）
- **例句**：Heightened **vigilance** is required when sharing personal identification on public domains.
- **译文**：在公共领域分享个人身份信息时，需要提高警惕。

### 4. Cultivate

- **音标**：/ˈkʌltɪveɪt/
- **词类**：v.
- **释义**：通过努力培养、获取或发展（能力、品质、关系等）
- **搭配**：`cultivate critical thinking`（培养批判性思维）；`cultivate professional networks`（建立职业人脉）
- **例句**：Higher education institutions aim to **cultivate** students' ability to synthesize complex information.
- **译文**：高等教育机构旨在培养学生综合复杂信息的能力。

### 5. Harness

- **音标**：/ˈhɑːrnɪs/
- **词类**：v.
- **释义**：控制并利用（自然力、资源或技术等）
- **搭配**：`harness collective intelligence`（利用集体智慧）；`harness technological potential`（发挥技术潜力）
- **例句**：Researchers are exploring innovative ways to **harness** big data for public health improvements.
- **译文**：研究人员正在探索利用大数据改善公共卫生的创新方法。

### 6. Deluge

- **音标**：/ˈdeljuːdʒ/
- **词类**：n. / v.
- **释义**：大量的涌现；淹没
- **搭配**：`a deluge of data`（数据洪流）；`be deluged with requests`（收到大量的请求）
- **例句**：The rapid proliferation of online content has resulted in a **deluge** of misinformation.
- **译文**：在线内容的快速增殖导致了误导性信息的泛滥。

### 7. Permeate ⭐ 新增

- **音标**：/ˈpɜːrmieɪt/
- **词类**：v.
- **释义**：渗透，弥漫，扩散到（某事物的）各个部分
- **搭配**：`permeate every aspect of life`（渗透到生活的方方面面）；`permeate the culture`（弥漫于文化之中）
- **例句**：Digital technology has **permeated** the educational landscape, transforming the way students learn.
- **译文**：数字技术已经渗透到教育领域，改变了学生的学习方式。
